# inequantiles 0.1.0

* Initial CRAN submission.
* Functions for weighted quantile estimation (`csquantile()`).
* Quantile-based inequality indicators: quantile ratio index (`qri()`), 
  share ratio (`share_ratio()`), percentile ratios (`ratio_quantiles()`), all indicators 
  and their standard error (`inequantiles()`).
* Influence functions for linearization of indicators (`*if_` functions)
* Rescaled bootstrap for complex survey designs (`rescaled_bootstrap()`).
* Functions and indicators for grouped data (quantiles, QRI, Gini - `*_grouped()` functions)
* Survey synthetic data (`synthouse`)
